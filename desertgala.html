Desert Gala – Abu Dhabi

Step into a world of elegance and Arabian charm at our Desert Gala in Abu Dhabi. Nestled within the golden sands, this event captures the true essence of a royal desert celebration.

Event Highlights

  • Location: Abu Dhabi Desert Dunes
  • Date: Custom dates available upon request
  • Time: Sunset to Midnight
  • Ambience: Ambient firelight, luxe Arabian décor, and flowing canopies under the stars
  • Entertainment: Live bands, belly dancers, traditional Tanoura show, and cultural drumming sessions
  • Food & Drink: Lavish Middle Eastern buffet with gourmet grills, desserts, and cocktails
  • Special Feature: Grand finale fireworks lighting up the desert sky
